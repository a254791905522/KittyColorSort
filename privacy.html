Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Kitty Color Sort handles information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Kitty Color Sort is designed as a lightweight offline puzzle game. The app does not require account registration and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

Local Game Data

The app stores limited gameplay information on your device to support normal features, including level progress, earned charm ratings, and music preference settings. This information is stored locally on the device and is not used to identify you personally.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

Based on the current version of the app in this project, no third-party analytics SDK, advertising SDK, or cross-app tracking feature is included.

Children's Privacy

The game is presented in a family-friendly style. The app is not intended to collect personal data from children or any other users.

Third-Party Services

If future versions add third-party tools or online services, this Privacy Policy may be updated to describe those changes. Platform providers such as Apple may process information under their own terms and privacy policies when you download or use apps through their services.

Data Security

Because gameplay data is stored locally on the device, your information remains under the protection of your device and operating system security settings.

Your Choices

You can clear locally stored gameplay data by removing the app from your device, subject to any operating system backup behavior.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, the revised version will be posted with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the app publisher through the support channel listed on the product page.
